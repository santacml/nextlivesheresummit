SparkUC + Mortar

Mortar Cincinnati represents the spirit of social innovation and entrepreneurship that the Innovation Initiative wishes to capture through its partnership with Next Lives Here and its second edition of Spark UC. Mortar's mission is centrally focused on targeting redeveloping and underserved communities and empowering their residents to take part in Cincinnati's revitalization through entrepreneurship. One of the central themes of the University of Cincinnati's Creating Our Third Century plan was a call for a focused and sustained investment in people. As students, we believe it is important to drive these initiatives forward, and we also believe an investment in people extends to not only the university's many stakeholders but also to those who reside in our larger Cincinnati community. Through this year's edition of Spark UC, interdisciplinary student teams will come together to try and figure out how to aid the entrepreneurs and businesses that Mortar serves.
